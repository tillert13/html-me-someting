Video Games

Like most children across the world; I grew up playing video games. Whenever I had free time I was almost always in my bedroom, surrounded by junk food, with a controller in my hands. One of my earliest childhood memories is when i had a sleepover at a friends house when I was 8, and we stayed up until about 3 in the morning having our first "Ultimate Video Game Tournament". What I would play vaired thoughout the years, but some of my childhood favorites included Jak and Daxter, Super Mario Sunshine, and Tekken.

Pokemon

Out of all of the games that I played throught my youth,the one the is dearest to my heart has to be Pokemon. I started playing when I was 7 and never looked back. I currently play the trading card game competitively, and actually attended the world championships in Washington DC back in August.

Basketball

Even though all I wanted to do was play video games, when I turned 10 my mom thought that it was best for me to be more active and convinced me to tryout for the basketball team. Despite my convincing size, I was never a great athlete and only play for a year, but in just that short year I made many friends, some of which I am still close with today.

Boy Scouts

After I decided to quit playnig basketbell, I found out the a few of the guys on the team were actually in Boy Scouts. I had never really known anything about the program and decided to look into it. After a few Google searches and asking around, I signed up. I stuck with the Scouts for about 5 years and climbed to the rank of Life Scout.

Football

Football is actually a sport I joined on a complete whim. I never enjoyed it when I was a kid, but when I was a sophmore in highschool, my friends said that I should try. One quick tryout later and next thing you know I was a juniour varsity running back.

Boxing

I was always interested in "Combat Sports" growing up. I watched wresting and UFC and enjoyed them, but never thought that that would be something I would ever want to do, but a few years ago my cousin opened his own personal gym and asked me to come check it out. Little did I know that he was going to convince me to try boxing. I was nervous, but gave it a try, and now i love it. I never actually spar, but working with a heavy bag or shadow boxing is a great way to stay in shape while also having fun.
